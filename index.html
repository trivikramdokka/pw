Trivikram (Vikram) Dokka

 

 

I'm an Assistant Professor (Lecturer) in Management Science Department at Lancaster University Management School. Within Management Science, I am associated to Optimization and Health Systems research groups. 

 

I am a member of Lancaster Data Science Institute and Centre for Transport and Logistics (CENTRAL).

 

I am academic member of STOR-i Center for Doctoral Training

 

 

Research Interests

Optimization (mainly combinatorial/discrete), Operations research and Business analytics, Data Science
